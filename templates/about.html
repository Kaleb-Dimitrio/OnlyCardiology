OnlyCardiology

OnlyCardiology is a project developed by five students from the Institute of Technology Sepuluh Nopember. This project aims to predict heart disease using machine learning techniques. The primary goal is to assist healthcare professionals in diagnosing heart conditions by providing accurate predictions based on a variety of medical parameters.


By leveraging datasets that include factors such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and other health indicators, the project employs machine learning models like Logistic Regression to predict the likelihood of heart disease. The models are trained and evaluated to ensure high accuracy and reliability in real-world scenarios.
